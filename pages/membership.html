  • What is a Foundation?

    • A foundation is a legal entity, often a nonprofit corporation or charitable trust, established to provide financial support for charitable purposes. It acts as a grant-making entity, distributing funds to other organizations, institutions, or individuals to support various causes like education, science, culture, and religion. Foundations can be either private (funded by a single source) or public (receiving support from various sources).
